3. Keep only self-improvement-relevant items.
4. Output 3-5 items with:
- What
- Why it matters for self-improvement
- Takeaway
- Relevance stars
5. Add one concrete experiment.
6. Add mandatory setup-review suggestions tied to findings.
7. Append posted items + experiment to tracking JSON.
